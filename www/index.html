Trends come and go. Good design remains a constant.

Mobile First

Over 60% of all initial views of a website are on mobile now. If you're site is not mobile ready, then you're losing views and business. Mobile phones have taken over. I for one welcome our new overlords.

Minimalist Design

Gone are the days of cluttered shotgun advertising that lists everything all at once, hoping for a hit. Clean sites that utilize intuitive cues, sliders, and bold keywords are the hallmarks of a modern website.

Responsive

Mobile tall. Mobile landscape. Tablets. Desktops. Technology necessitates a site to be responsive in how it looks on modern devices. If your site loses functionality because it isn't responsive, you're losing $.
